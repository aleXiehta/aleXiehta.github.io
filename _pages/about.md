Tsun-An Hsieh is a Ph.D. student who joined [SAIGE](https://saige.sice.indiana.edu/), Indiana University (IU), in 2022. Before joining IU, he worked as a research assistant in [Bio-ASP Lab](https://bio-asplab.citi.sinica.edu.tw/index.html) at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. Before then, Tsun-An received his M.S. degree in Networking and Multimedia at National Taiwan University and B.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at Chang Gung University. His research interests include transfer learning, metric learning, and causal inference in machine learning. As a student in the ISE department, Tsun-An is proficient in incorporating modern machine learning algorithms into conventional speech and audio tasks, such as speech enhancement, dereverberation, and synthesis.
