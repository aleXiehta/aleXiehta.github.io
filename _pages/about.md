Tsun-An Hsieh is currently a Ph.D. student joined in [SAIGE](https://saige.sice.indiana.edu/), Indiana University (IU) in 2022. Before joining IU, he worked as a research assistant in [Bio-ASP Lab](https://bio-asplab.citi.sinica.edu.tw/index.html) led by [Dr. Yu Tsao](https://www.citi.sinica.edu.tw/pages/yu.tsao/index_en.html) at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. Before then, Tsun-An received his MS degree in Networking and Multimedia at National Taiwan University and BS degree in Computer Science at Chang Gung University. Tsun-An's research interests include transfer learning, metric learning, and causal inference in machine learning. As a student in the ISE department, Tsun-An is proficient in incorporating modern machine learning algorithms into conventional speech and audio tasks, such as speech enhancement, dereverberation, and synthesis.
